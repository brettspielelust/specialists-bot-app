Unofficial fan-made Flamme Rouge App to play a Single Race or Grand Tour with Specialist Riders (Bots).

Disclaimer: This is an unofficial, fan-made web app intended strictly for private, non-commercial use. I am not commercially associated with the game's author or publisher. All credits for the Flamme Rouge board game belong to designer Asger Aleksandrov Granerud and publisher Lautapelit.

This web app is based on a fan-made solo version published on BoardGameGeek by Fredrik Stahre. Fredrik's aim was to make the Flamme Rouge Bots use all the new rules included in the Grand Tour expansion (specialist powers).

Since playing this solo variant requires printing and cutting out a lot of cards, taking up table space and adding administrative overhead, I developed this app. It digitally simulates the drawing and playing of Energy Cards for Specialist Riders (Bots), allowing you to compete against up to five teams. In addition to a Single Race, you can play a Grand Tour (where half of each Bot's Exhaustion Cards are carried over to the next stage).

Please note: This app uses your browser's local storage so you can resume your game later (no login is required, and no data is saved to external databases!). The code for this app is licensed under Creative Commons and is available on GitHub. Feel free to adopt or enhance this project.

On the setup screen, choose whether you want to ride a Single Race or embark on a multi-stage Grand Tour.

Next, select the number of competing Bot Teams. Assign a color to each team and recruit their Specialist Riders. Every team must consist of exactly one Rouleur and one Sprinteur. You may recruit the same Specialist type across multiple teams.

Click "START RACE" to begin the first round.

During the race, select a Rider in turn order (starting from the front of the peloton) to open their action panel. Click "Draw Card" to reveal their Energy Card, and decide if a second card is required according to the rules..

Resolve all Bots in turn order. At the end of the round, distribute Exhaustion Cards to the affected Riders before proceeding to the next round.
